Join Us!

In order to join our society online, you need to be a student at UCL. You can sign up here.

Why join us?

Ice Hockey

  • We provide the cheapest ice time in London at £10 for an hour and a half of training every Wednesday run by an experienced coach.
  • Experienced players can play in the British University Ice Hockey Division 1, as part of the A team.
  • We also offer specific training sessions for beginners at Sobell Ice and Leisure Centre.
  • Locker access at Streatham Ice Rink for the team - no more lugging your kit around London!
  • Free team bus to all away games.
  • Unique London Ice Varsity against Imperial College London.
  • Weekly Socials!

Figure Skating

  • It is the best value for money with lessons and ice time for only £7 a week (includes 3 hours of ice time and half an hour of group coached lesson).
  • For experienced skaters, we can provide cheap on and off ice training (please enquire for more information).
  • We also have a unique varsity performance taking place once a year.
  • We have the Ice Factor (a figure skating performance for all skill levels!).
  • Weekly Socials!
